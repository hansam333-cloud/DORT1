## Product

## Feedback Summary

The product's main fit value is being understood by customers. Reviews mention shoulder, arm, and upper-body line positively. The main friction points are size uncertainty between L and XL, white transparency, and slight color expectation gap.

## Positive Signals

- Shoulder and arm fit are clearly recognized.
- Customers wearing usual 105 respond well to L.
- XL works well for customers who prefer a more relaxed daily fit.
- Neckline quality appears trustworthy at first impression.
- The product has content value around arm line and upper-body balance.

## Negative Signals

- Some customers expected a tighter body fit.
- White color may need clearer transparency guidance.
- Color accuracy in photos may need improvement.
- L vs XL confusion is likely to repeat.

## Size and Fit Insights

Main pattern:

```text
L = cleaner physical fit
XL = relaxed daily fit
```

Recommended update:

- Add a size comparison block between L and XL.
- Add model fit notes for both regular and relaxed fit preference.
- Add customer-facing wording that the product is semi-physical fit, not tight compression fit.

## Fabric and Quality Insights

White transparency should be explained more clearly.

Recommended update:

```text
화이트 컬러는 밝은 이너 착용 시 비침이 적지만, 어두운 이너와 함께 착용하면 약간의 비침이 느껴질 수 있습니다.
```

Neckline feedback is positive but needs longer-term review tracking.
