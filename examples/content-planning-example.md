```text

Main material/detail benefit: Soft cotton-blend fabric with slight stretch.
Recommended styling: Wide denim, semi-wide slacks, bermuda shorts.
Expected selling price: 37,900 KRW

Goal: Launch awareness and product page traffic
Platform: Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Feed
Content type: Short-form videos and feed carousel posts
Posting period: 7 days

Available assets:
Model fit shots, upper-body close-up, detail cuts, fabric touch shot.

Target customer concern:
Customers worry that muscle-fit t-shirts are too tight, too gym-like, or make the waist look bulky.
```
