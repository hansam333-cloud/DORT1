# DORT Brand Guide

## Brand Position

DORT is a men's fashion brand for men who want their athletic or better-than-average body type to look natural, balanced, and refined in everyday outfits.

The brand should feel:

- Minimal, but not plain
- Physical, but not gym-heavy
- Direct, but not exaggerated
- Practical, but still premium
- Korean men's shopping mall friendly, but more structured and trustworthy

## Core Message

```text
Physical fit dailywear.
```

Korean expression:

```text
운동한 체형과 남자다운 비율이 자연스럽게 살아나는 피지컬핏 데일리웨어.
```

## Fit Philosophy

DORT does not sell clothes only by item category. DORT sells body-balance outcomes.

Core fit points:

- Shoulders look wider
- Arms look naturally filled
- Chest is visible but not tight
- Waist does not look bulky
- Length supports clean full-body proportions
- Full-body proportion looks clean

## Tone of Voice

Use clear, confident, practical language.

Good:

- "183cm 운동 체형 기준으로 검수했습니다."
- "어깨 라인이 자연스럽게 넓어 보이는 핏입니다."
- "총장이 짧게 뜨지 않도록 잡았습니다."
- "운동한 체형은 살리고, 과하게 붙는 느낌은 줄였습니다."

Avoid:

- Overly loud claims
- Cheap-looking hype
- Too many exclamation marks
- Unverifiable claims
- Body-shaming language

Avoid examples:

- "어깨 미친듯이 넓어보임!!!"
- "입으면 무조건 몸 좋아보임"
- "여자들이 좋아하는 옷"

## Visual Direction

Preferred:

- Clean studio background
- Full-body fit verification shots
- Upper-body fit close-ups
- Detail cuts for neckline, shoulder, sleeve, hem, fabric
- Realistic proportions
- Neutral, grounded colors with restrained accents

Avoid:

- Overly dark or blurred imagery
- Random decorative graphics
- Stock-like poses with unclear fit
- Excessive gym mood
- Cropped photos that hide full-body proportion

## Competitor Benchmark Notes

Use competitors selectively:

- Fromtome: styling curation and outfit linkage
- Cosyharu: direct body-benefit copy
- VIPman: muscular-fit demand and review trust signals
- Deiro: clean product information structure

DORT should combine these into a more trustworthy physical-fit system.

## Product Naming Formula

```text
[Target/Fit USP] Product Name
```

Examples:

- `[피지컬핏] 쿨링 머슬 반팔티`
- `[어깨라인 보정] 세미크롭 링거 티셔츠`
- `[183 기준핏] 롱암 오버 셔츠`
- `[롱레그핏] 세미와이드 데님`
